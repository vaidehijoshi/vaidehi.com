Vaidehi is an engineering leader, writer, and keynote speaker with over a decade of experience in software engineering. She has led as both an engineering manager and a senior individual contributor, with a track record of architecting complex systems, building high-performing teams, and making technical ideas accessible at scale.

She created BaseCS and BaseDS — companion series on computer science and distributed systems fundamentals — along with the BaseCS video series (season 1 & season 2) and the Byte Sized video series on YouTube. She co-hosted the Base.cs Podcast for 9 seasons, reaching over 1.5 million downloads globally.

She has spoken at conferences around the world — including RailsConf, EmberConf, and LeadDev — delivering both keynotes and technical talks.
